Pasta Carbonara

What you need

Ingredient Amount
Olive oi 1 tablespoon
Bacon 200g
Whole eggs 3-4
Garlic cloves 1 teaspoon
Parmesan 1 cup
Spaghetti 500g
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
  1. Heat pasta water
  2. While the water is coming to a boil, heat the olive oil or butter in a large saut√© pan over medium heat. Add the bacon or pancetta and cook slowly until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and half of the cheese
  4. Once the water has reached a rolling boil, add the dry pasta, and cook, uncovered, at a rolling boil.
  5. Toss pasta with bacon
  6. Add the beaten eggs with cheese and toss quickly to combine once more.
  7. Serve at once with the rest of the parmesan and freshly ground black pepper. If you want, sprinkle with a little fresh chopped parsley.
